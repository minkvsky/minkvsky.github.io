### Vehicle Detection and Tracking

https://gitlab.com/CARND-PROJECTS/CarND-Vehicle-Detection

- goals: 在给定视频中检测车辆和车道线
- 车道线检测

    - Use color transforms, gradients, etc., to create a thresholded binary image.
